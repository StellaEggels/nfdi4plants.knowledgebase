<ViolaSays>
I think my ARC is now ready for publication.
How can I submit my ARC for a data publication? 
</ViolaSays>

<Card icon="gitlab" title="PLANTDataHUB">
    <Steps>

    1. Sign in to the [PLANTDataHUB](https://git.nfdi4plants.org)
    2. Navigate to the ARC you want to publish
    3. <div style="display: flex;"> In the right sidebar click on the "Invenio" badge <img src={invenio.src} style="padding-left:10px"/> </div>
    4. You are automatically redirected to the DataPLANT Publication Service (https://archigator.nfdi4plants.org).
    4. Login with your DataPLANT account
    5. A summary of the ARC is shown including the investigation metadata.
    6. You can follow the steps in the right sidebar to publish your ARC.
        
        :::note
        Please avoid to request a publication for your demo ARC ðŸ˜‰
        :::
    </Steps>
</Card>

:::tip
Before you can publish your ARC to DataPLANT's publication service, it needs to be [validated](/nfdi4plants.knowledgebase/start-here/validate-arc) for Invenio.
:::
